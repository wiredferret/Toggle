Who gets to see everything? Use roles to sort people, instead of manually adding them. Role-based access control means that an administrator's ability to change the code is derived from the same place as their permissions to control other parts of your infrastructure. Setting a new administrator's security and privileges is as simple as adding them to a group with designated roles. For Toggle, this means that all scouts can access any other scout's ship, in case of emergencies, but Toggle doesn't have the access to turn on the signal flags - only SpaceLanes HQ can do that.
